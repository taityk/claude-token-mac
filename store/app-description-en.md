# App Store Description (English)

## App Name
TokenBar

## Subtitle (30 chars max)
Claude.ai usage in your menu bar

## Description (4000 chars max)

Never be caught off guard by Claude.ai's usage limits again.

TokenBar sits quietly in your macOS menu bar and shows your remaining Claude.ai capacity at a glance — no app switching, no surprises.

FEATURES

● Always-visible menu bar display
Your remaining usage appears as a percentage right in the menu bar. It's always there when you need it.

● Three-state status indicator
· Normal (default): plenty of capacity remaining
· Warning (orange): running low — time to wrap up
· Limited (red): shows a live countdown to your next reset, updated every second

● Automatic refresh
TokenBar checks your usage every minute automatically. Tap "Refresh Now" any time for an instant update.

● Visual progress bar
Click the menu bar item to see a progress bar alongside the percentage — easy to read at a glance.

● Simple login flow
Sign in to Claude.ai the same way you always do, in a native browser window. Your session is stored securely in the macOS Keychain — TokenBar never sees your password.

PERFECT FOR
· Daily Claude.ai users who want to stay ahead of their limits
· Anyone who has been interrupted mid-task by an unexpected usage cap
· Developers, writers, and researchers who rely on Claude.ai throughout the day
